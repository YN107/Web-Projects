National

The Languages Track at Al Ahfad School offers a diverse curriculum immersing students in various languages and cultures. It provides comprehensive instruction in language acquisition, linguistics, and cultural studies, enabling students to become proficient in multiple languages. This program fosters communication skills, cultural awareness, and appreciation for linguistic diversity.

Arabic

The Arabic Track at Al Ahfad School offers a comprehensive curriculum focused on the Arabic language, including its grammar, literature, and cultural contexts. Designed for students who wish to gain proficiency in Arabic and explore its rich heritage, this track provides in-depth instruction and immersive experiences. Students develop strong language skills and a deep appreciation for the rich cultural and literary traditions of the Arabic-speaking world.

Internatioal

The International Languages Track at Al Ahfad School is designed for students aspiring to become global citizens by mastering multiple languages and understanding diverse cultures. This track offers an immersive curriculum covering language acquisition, intercultural communication, and global perspectives. It prepares students for careers in international relations, global business, and diplomacy, equipping them with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in a multicultural and interconnected world.

Playground

Al Ahfad School boasts a well-maintained football field, serving as a central hub for students' physical activities and sports events. The field features high-quality turf, goalposts, and seating areas. It provides an excellent space for football matches, practice sessions, and various sports activities, promoting physical fitness and teamwork. The school's commitment to sports is evident through the regular maintenance of the field, ensuring it remains a valuable asset.

Pool

Al Ahfad School features a modern, well-equipped swimming pool, enhancing its commitment to physical education and extracurricular activities. Safety measures, including lifeguards, ensure a secure environment for students. The area includes changing rooms and spectator seating, making it a hub for aquatic events and recreational swimming. This facility underscores the school's dedication to providing a well-rounded educational experience with opportunities for physical fitness and skill development.
